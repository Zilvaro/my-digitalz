As a company that handles private data submitted to us by EU/Swiss data subjects we observe the full ramifications of EU privacy law (GDPR) and Swiss Data Protection Act (SDPA).

We are committed to the responsibilities placed on us as a processor of data and observant of the eight primary rights guaranteed by the GDPR and SDPA:


  1. The Right to Be Informed
  2. The Right of Access
  3. The Right to Rectification
  4. The Right to Erasure
  5. The Right to Restrict Processing
  6. The Right to Data Portability
  7. The Right to Object
  8. The Right to Avoid Automated Decision-Making
