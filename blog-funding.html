Why DigitalZ Funding

Let’s be clear: we understand there are a lot of crowdfunding platforms, including ones of major social-media networks and financial institutions.

We have looked at most of them and identified where they excel and what they miss for the users to benefit the most in their funding activities. Here are 7 main reasons why we think DigitalZ is an excellent choice for you:


  1. DigitalZ has continuous engagement with Your supporters. It is not limited in time or a project. You can communicate with different groups of friends, partners, volunteers, donators for as long as you wish to continue your initiative.

  2. DigitalZ is personalized for you. The colours, layouts, content give users the feeling they use your proprietary app. At the same time, you use the benefits of being in a larger platform since donators from other sustainability communities have a chance noticing your name and activities for support.

  3. You receive all the donations. There is no “All-or-nothing” rule as in many other crowdfunding platforms when you receive the money if you exceed the set funding objective or receive nothing.

  4. DigitalZ is a free advertisement platform for your activities. You can continuously link videos, place sustainability workshops materials, product or service descriptions, event advertisement or even the products on sale.

  5. You can link it to the external sites or information. You can easily link the application to your e-shops, partners’ offers to enrich the proposals to your supporters.

  6. Notifications. Your application is on the individual’s phone with regular notifications and reminders about your activities. You can regulate the frequency and context of the messages to different user groups of your application.

  7. Chat or send some tasks to perform. You may use the crowd-engagement function to deliver the tasks to your team members, partners, influencers, donators, volunteers and control the execution.


So, if you want a continuous personalized engagement with your supporters to grow the success of your initiative, DigitalZ shall be your aid!
