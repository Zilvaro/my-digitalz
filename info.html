DigitalZ is your app!

The solution was born from the desire to make technology accessible to the small enterprises, associations and ambitious individuals who have great ideas but lack funds or knowledge to equip themselves with digital tools.

Swiss digital consulting company Digital Case Sàrl has develop a set of tools for the benefit of aiding people working in Green Economy and Assisted Living fields.

With this as a mission Don4Bon serves a great variety of projects and initiatives:

Ones are the foundations, associations and other types of non-profit organizations that can use the platform as a continuous personalized communication and fund-raising application. It also allows to organize volunteers, manage events & workshops, and maintain a regular conversation with supporters.

Others are energetic start-ups and SME in the areas of energy, sustainability or safety deploying our platform to efficiently digitize their engagement with clients and communities, benefiting from big-data and targeted communication.
