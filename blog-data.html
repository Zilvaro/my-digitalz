Use data to grow

DigitalZ provides you data & support how to use it

Numbers without an idea what you want to see are meaningless. We help you to find the path in analytics and the data ‘jungle’.

3 analytical models can transform a vast amount of information about a market, products & services, users’ behaviour, preferences, or community activity to the insights & ideas of what you ought to do to grow the success of your activities:


  1. Behaviour Change Formula or Engagement Success.
    Digital transformation is all about people start doing things differently or in other words – they change their behaviour.

  2. Value Model or Benefits of Digital.
    It helps you to identify where you can generate the real revenue or savings by using digital engagement. How to forecast and measure that.

  3. Analysis Flow Model or Data to Insights.
    How to construct the flow of data about activities, behaviours, drivers into a story describing your business or activity results.

Upon your request we conduct the ‘light’ workshops on those concepts and models, manage the personalized set-up projects and provide data analysis service.
